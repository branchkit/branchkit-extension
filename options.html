Badge rules

Customize which elements get badges on specific sites. Excludes hide cluttering elements, includes pick up widgets the scanner misses, and reveals make hover-hidden elements visible. Rules sync across browsers.

Key rules

Per-site control over BranchKit’s keyboard shortcuts. Disable them entirely on sites that have their own keys, or pass just specific keys through (e.g. jke# for Gmail) while the rest of your shortcuts keep working. Patterns match like badge rules (*.example.com). Voice is unaffected. Sync across browsers.

Keyboard shortcuts

Rebind the in-page keyboard commands; each can have more than one key. Bare keys act as shortcuts in Normal mode — they pause while you're typing in a text field, or typing a badge codeword after f. Add a Ctrl/Cmd modifier if you want a shortcut to fire even while typing in a field. Shortcuts sync across browsers. Keybinding changes are staged — click Save to apply or Cancel to revert.
